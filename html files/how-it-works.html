How YANA–IGY Works

YANA–IGY delivers precision alerts during natural disasters — giving more time to reach safety.

  • Only phones in danger zone alert — no broad panic.
  • Large green arrow points exact safe path.
  • Works offline — mesh phone-to-phone.
  • Inclusion for vulnerable — voice, vibration, larger text.
