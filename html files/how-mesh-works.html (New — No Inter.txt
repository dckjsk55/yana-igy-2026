    <h1>How the YANA–IGY Mesh Works</h1>
    <p>The mesh is purpose-built for disasters — <strong>not general internet</strong>.</p>
    <p><strong>No</strong> browsing websites, streaming, or full web access.</p>
    <h2>What the Mesh Does</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Sends YANA–IGY alerts (crimson screen, green arrow).</li>
      <li>Short text messages (“I’m safe”, “Need help”).</li>
      <li>Sensor data sharing.</li>
    </ul>
    <h2>Why No Full Internet</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>Low bandwidth — designed for life-saving.</li>
      <li>Security — prevents misuse.</li>
    </ul>
    <p>When online — normal internet returns. Mesh ready for blackout.</p>
